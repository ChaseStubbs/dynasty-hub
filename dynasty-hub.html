    ? Dynasty Hub — Help Guide
    
        
    Everything you need to know to get the most out of the hub.
    
      
    
      
    
    
    
    

      
    
        
    
          
    ⚙️Getting Started & Setup
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    Open ⚙ Setup in the top-right to set your team name, conference, season number, and current ranking.
    
          
    Team Color: Search your school in the color field — a dropdown shows matching teams with color swatches. Click to select. The entire app repaints to your team's color. If your school isn't listed, use the color picker on the right to enter a custom hex.
    
          
    Team Logo: Upload any PNG or JPG from your device. It appears in the header next to your team name. If no logo is uploaded, the hub shows your team's initials in a colored badge instead.
    
          
    Tip: Your logo is stored locally in your browser — it won't be included in the JSON export file. Re-upload it if you switch devices or browsers.
    
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    
          
    📋Game Planner
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    The planner is built around your season schedule. Select a game from the Select a Game dropdown and the week and opponent auto-fill. Selecting a game also pre-seeds the Analysis matchup card with that opponent automatically.
    
          
    Key Points are your session checklist. When you select a game, your active Storylines appear as one-tap chips above the key points list — click any to add it instantly. Add your own points by typing and pressing Enter or clicking + Add.
    
          
    Pre-Game Notes are for prep. Post-Game Notes capture what happened. When you're done, click 📋 Log Session to save and reset for the next game. Schedule games with a logged session show a 🎬 badge in the Season Schedule.
    
          
    Off-season types: Signing Day, Portal Window, Spring Ball, Preseason, and Off-Season Update are available for work between seasons. Select "Off-season / No game" from the game picker.
    
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    
          
    📅Season Schedule
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    Build your schedule at the start of each season using + Add Game at the top of the Game Log tab. Set the week, opponent, location, game type (Rivalry, Bowl, Playoff, etc.), and difficulty.
    
          
    Once you log a game result in the game log, that week's schedule row automatically updates with a green (W) or red (L) result and score.
    
          
    New Season: The schedule clears automatically when you archive a season — no need to delete entries manually.
    
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    
          
    🏈Game Log
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    Log each game after you play it. The W–L record in the header is always derived from your game log — never a manual counter, so it's always accurate.
    
          
    The Season Trajectory chart plots your ranking over time using the "Ranking at Kickoff" field — fill that in for each game to get a meaningful chart.
    
          
    To edit a logged game, click the ✎ button on any game row. The form fills in and the button changes to "💾 Update Game". Click ✕ Cancel to discard changes.
    
          
    🏆 Heisman Watch lives in the Game Log tab — add candidates and update their stats week to week to track their campaign arc.
    
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    
          
    ⭐Recruiting
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    Track every prospect on your board. Statuses run: Target → Offered → Visited → Verbal → Committed → Signed → Flipped. Update status inline from the dropdown on each row.
    
          
    NIL Offer (DP): Enter how many Dynasty Points you're offering. These feed directly into the Blueprint DP budget under "Recruit NIL."
    
          
    💎 Diamond in the Rough: Flag low-star recruits you believe will outperform their rating. They show in the Diamond Finder scatter chart in Player Development.
    
          
    → Dev (mid-season push): Once a recruit is Committed or Signed in-game, a green → Dev button appears on their card. Click it, enter a starting OVR, and they move straight to the Prospect Pipeline in Player Development — no need to wait for end-of-season Finalize Class. They stay on the recruiting board until Finalize Class cleans them up.
    
          
    Finalize Class: At season's end, click 🎓 Finalize Class in the Development tab. Players already pushed via → Dev are skipped automatically (no duplicates) and removed from the board.
    
          
    Tip: Transfer portal targets live on the separate Portal tab — not here. Keep high school recruits on this board.
    
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    
          
    🚪Transfer Portal
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    The Portal board is entirely separate from recruiting. Use it to track players you're pursuing through the transfer portal.
    
          
    Set the Window (Winter or Summer) to keep portal cycles organized. Filter the board by window, active targets, signed, or lost.
    
          
    Once a portal target is Committed or Signed, a green → Dev button appears on their card — same as recruiting. Click it to push them directly to the Prospect Pipeline in Player Development mid-season.
    
          
    Portal targets you sign also appear in 🎓 Finalize Class under a separate "Portal Transfers" section at season's end. Players already pushed via → Dev are skipped automatically.
    
          
    Portal NIL offers feed into the Blueprint budget under "Recruit NIL" alongside your high school offers.
    
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    
          
    📐Program & Blueprint
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    The Blueprint tracks your Dynasty Point (DP) budget. DP is use-it-or-lose-it in CFB27 — allocate it wisely each season.
    
          
    Click Edit Budget to set your total DP and manually allocate to Recruiting, Staff, and Facilities. Recruit NIL and Roster NIL calculate automatically from what you've entered on those boards.
    
          
    The Remaining counter turns red if you've over-allocated — a warning to adjust before the season locks in.
    
          
    🎯 AD Expectations: Your Athletic Director sets 3 goals each season. Click Edit Goals to enter them, then mark them complete as you hit them. Meeting all 3 builds program trust.
    
          
    Staff and Facilities are tracked in the Program tab. Log coaches with their archetype and facilities with DP cost so you have a record for the show.
    
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    
          
    💎Player Development
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    Your full roster lives here after players are finalized from recruiting or portal. Track OVR, role, year, and development arc for each player.
    
          
    Prospect Pipeline: Players pushed mid-season via the → Dev button on the recruiting or portal boards land here first — in a gold-highlighted Pipeline section above your active roster. They're committed but not yet officially enrolled. Hit Enroll → on a pipeline card to move them to your Active Roster, or run Finalize Class at season's end to enroll everyone at once.
    
          
    Source badges: Every player card shows a small badge — Recruit (green), Portal (blue), or Manual (gray) — so you always know how they got on your roster.
    
          
    OVR History: Click the chart icon on a player to log OVR snapshots over time (pre-season, mid-season, etc.) — great for showing growth arcs on the show.
    
          
    Breakout Moments: Log specific games or moments where a player broke out. These attach to the player card as a storyline record.
    
          
    💎 Diamond Finder chart: Scatter plot of stars recruited vs. current OVR. Players above the baseline outperformed their recruiting ranking — your true diamonds.
    
          
    📅 Advance Year: At the end of each season, use this to bump every player up one year (FR→SO, etc.). SR+ players stay capped.
    
          
    💸 Roster NIL Demands: Log when players demand NIL contracts. Set the demand amount and status (Pending/Accepted/Declined/Renegotiating). Accepted demands feed into the Blueprint's Roster NIL line automatically.
    
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    
          
    🗂Depth Chart
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    The 🗂 Depth Chart tab lets you set a 3-deep depth chart for every position — Offense, Defense, and Special Teams. It works from Season 1 — just type any player name directly. No roster needed.
    
          
    Each position row has three text input slots: #1 Starter, #2 Backup, and #3. Type freely or, once you have enrolled players in 💎 Development, their names appear as autocomplete suggestions. All entries save automatically when you tab or click away.
    
          
    The needs indicator at the top tracks unfilled starter slots and highlights those rows in red — useful for spotting recruiting gaps by position. As you fill starters the indicator updates live.
    
          
    If you delete a player from Development, any depth chart slot that stored their name via the Dev roster will be cleared automatically.
    
          
    Tip: Keep the Depth Chart tab open alongside Game Planner — it's a two-second reference for your two-deep at every position before you record or play.
    
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    
          
    📰Universe News
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    Log notable things happening around your virtual college football universe — upsets, Top 25 movement, coaching carousel moves, big portal signings from rival schools.
    
          
    This gives you context to reference when planning sessions or tracking your dynasty narrative — and builds the lore of your dynasty world over time.
    
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    
          
    📚Season Archive & New Season
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    At the end of each season, click 📅 New Season in the header. This archives the season with your final record, rank, recruits, NIL total, and AD goals, then resets for the next season.
    
          
    What resets: Game log, schedule, news feed, AD goals, current episode, recruiting board (unsigned players removed), portal board, NIL demands, Heisman watch.
    
          
    What carries over: Roster (players), coaching staff, facilities, and Blueprint budget.
    
          
    View past seasons anytime by expanding 📚 Season History at the bottom of the Game Log tab.
    
          
    Important: Run 🎓 Finalize Class and 📅 Advance Year in Player Development before archiving the season so your roster is current.
    
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    
          
    📈Analysis — Matchup Card & Season Comparison
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    This Week's Matchup is a broadcast-style reference card at the top of the Analysis tab. Enter your upcoming opponent's name, ranking, record, and key stats — the card renders a live side-by-side panel with your season averages on the left and theirs on the right.
    
          
    Green highlights show which side has the edge on each stat. It's designed to stay open on a second monitor or phone while you're recording or playing — quick reference without leaving the game.
    
          
    Tip: If you've filled out Team Offense and Defense in the Stats tab, your numbers populate automatically. The opponent fields are manual — look up their stats in-game or on the dynasty screen.
    
          
    Season Comparison lets you compare any two archived seasons side by side using ESPN-style mirrored bars. Offense and defense stats are shown with gold highlights on the better value per row. Rows where neither season has data are automatically hidden.
    
          
    Season Comparison unlocks once you've archived at least one season. Use the End of Season button in Game Log to archive.
    
          
    Player Comparison lets you pick any two players from any season — current or archived — and see their stat lines side by side. Players are added via the Stats tab. Once added, they appear in both dropdowns labeled by name, position, and season.
    
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    
          
    💾Saving, Export & Import
    
          ▶
        
    
        
    
          
    Everything saves automatically as you type or make changes — there's no save button to hit. Data lives in your browser's localStorage.
    
          
    💾 Export: Downloads a .json backup file of your entire dynasty. Do this regularly, especially before clearing your browser.
    
          
    📂 Import: Load a previously exported .json file to restore your dynasty on any device or browser.
    
          
    Warning: Clearing your browser's site data or cookies will erase your dynasty. Export regularly to avoid data loss. Your team logo is not included in the export — re-upload it after importing.
